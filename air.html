Air Pollution

Air pollution is a major environmental concern affecting millions of people worldwide. Here are some key facts about air pollution:


  • It is caused by harmful gases, particulate matter, and other pollutants released from vehicles, industries, and other sources.
  • Air pollution can lead to respiratory diseases, heart problems, and other health issues.
  • Reducing air pollution requires adopting cleaner energy sources, improving transportation systems, and implementing stricter environmental regulations.
  • Individuals can help by using public transportation, carpooling, reducing energy consumption, and supporting clean energy initiatives.
