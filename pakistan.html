Information Regarding Pakistan's Carbon Emissions

The graph shows the total amount of Carbon dioxide emissions conducted by Pakistan and types of fuel being used that emit the most. Oil throughout the year 2022 has been emitting the most levels of carbon dioxide with a total of 66 million tonnes, followed by gas with over 60 million tonnes of carbon dioxide.
