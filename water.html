Water Pollution

Water pollution is a pressing environmental issue with severe consequences. Here are some key facts about water pollution:


  • It occurs when harmful substances, such as chemicals, waste, or toxins, contaminate bodies of water like rivers, lakes, and oceans.
  • Water pollution can harm aquatic life, disrupt ecosystems, and affect human health through contaminated drinking water.
  • Sources of water pollution include industrial discharge, agricultural runoff, improper waste disposal, and oil spills.
  • Preventing water pollution requires implementing stricter regulations, improving wastewater treatment, promoting sustainable agriculture, and raising awareness about conservation.
