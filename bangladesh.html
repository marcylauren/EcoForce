Information Regarding India’s Carbon Emissions

The graph shows the total amount of Carbon dioxide emissions conducted by India and types of fuel being used that emit the most. Coal is the main source of the total emissions of carbon within India with almost 2 billion tonnes of Carbon Dioxide being emitted in the year 2022. Followed by oil with nearly 700 million tonnes of Carbon dioxide.Despite the recent drop throughout the year in 2022 the amount of Carbon dioxide being emitted from gas in Bangladesh is still a significant amount that shouldn’t be ignored.
